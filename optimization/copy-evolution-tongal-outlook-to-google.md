### Copy Changes Recommended

#### Email 1 — for tech/SaaS targets

**BEFORE (actual email sent to Jolanta Aerts at Vanta):**
```
Hi Jolanta,

Noticed that you recently started as the Creative Director at Vanta.

Brands like BetterHelp, Samsung and Education.com use Tongal to create broadcast quality video, animation, and social content through our community of 160,000 creators across 175 countries.

Faster than an agency, a fraction of the cost, and you own everything.

Worth exploring for Vanta?
```

**AFTER (suggested 2026-04-02):**
```
Hi {First Name},

Saw {Company}'s approach to {specific company observation}.

Tongal helped BetterHelp cut CPA by 35% with creator-driven testimonials that served 50M+ impressions. Samsung and Disney use us for broadcast and social content.

160,000 creators, 175 countries. You own everything.

Would it make sense to share a few relevant samples?
```

**What changed and why:**
- **Hook:** Removed "recently started as" (data often stale) → company-specific observation
- **Social proof:** Added BetterHelp metric (35% CPA cut, 50M impressions) — creative buyers want results, not just brand names
- **CTA:** `Worth exploring?` → `share a few relevant samples?` — directly addresses what Jolanta's reply asked for (portfolio). This CTA pre-empts the most common objection.
